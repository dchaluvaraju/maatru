# Mātṛ

## मातृ

---

## Symbolic Definition

**Mātṛ** is not merely “mother.”  
It is the **Form-Giving Intelligence**.

From:
- √mā — to measure, to delimit, to give proportion  
- -tṛ — the agent, the one who acts  

Mātṛ = **The Agent of Formation**

---

## Metaphysical Interpretation

Where there is undifferentiated potential,  
Mātṛ introduces boundary.

Where there is formlessness,  
Mātṛ introduces structure.

Where there is possibility,  
Mātṛ introduces embodiment.

Mātṛ is the principle that converts:
- Energy → Form  
- Idea → Structure  
- Consciousness → Creation  

---

## Archetypal Layer

- Cosmic Matrix  
- Creative Womb  
- Boundary-Setter  
- Embodiment Engine  

---

## Essence

> Mātṛ is the intelligence that measures the infinite into the finite.
